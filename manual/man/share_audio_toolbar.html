Sharing & Effects Toolbar

From Audacity Development Manual
Jump to: navigation, search
Sharing & Effects Toolbar has two buttons, enabling you to:
  1. share your audio on-line with others on the audio.com website
  2. download additional plugin effects


Share Audio button

You will need to create an audio.com account to participate in audio sharing.
  • Note carefully that this is a paid-for service and not free.

Clicking on the Share Audio button will bring up the following dialog, if you are not logged in on audio.com:

Once you have entered your Track Title you will get a further dialog inviting you to create an audio.com account or to login if you already have one.

For more details please see Sharing Audio Online on the Audacity Support website.


Get Effects button

This button is not available on Linux builds of Audacity as there are currently no Linux compatible plugins available.

Clicking on the Get Effects button will bring up the following dialog, which will enable you to download additional plugin effects:

Note carefully that not all of the plugins are free to download, some will cost money.
